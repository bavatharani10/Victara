A course is a unit of teaching that typically lasts one academic term, is led by one or more instructors, and has a fixed roster of students. A course usually covers an individual subject.

Intermediate

In the Indian education system, intermediate in college refers to a two-year pre-university course (PUC) or pre-degree course (PDC). It's also known as a junior college or intermediate college.

Degree

A college degree is a qualification that a student earns after completing a course of study at a college or university. Degrees are awarded at different levels, such as undergraduate and postgraduate

Post Graduation

A postgraduate degree is a higher-level qualification that students pursue after completing an undergraduate degree. Postgraduate degrees can include master's degrees, doctorates, and postgraduate diplomas.
